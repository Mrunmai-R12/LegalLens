MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of January 1, 2024
between Vendor X Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (“Service Provider”) and Client Y Corporation
(“Client”).

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 1 – Definitions

1.1 “Confidential Information” means any non-public information disclosed by either
party, including business strategies, technical data, customer information, and pricing.

1.2 “Services” means the professional services to be provided by the Service Provider
as described in applicable Statements of Work.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 2 – Scope of Services

2.1 The Service Provider shall perform the Services in accordance with industry
standards and applicable laws.

2.2 Any modification to the scope of Services shall require mutual written agreement
between the parties.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 3 – Payment Terms

3.1 The Client shall pay the Service Provider the fees specified in the Statement of Work.

3.2 All invoices shall be payable within thirty (30) days from the date of invoice.

3.3 Late payments may incur interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 4 – Confidentiality Obligations

4.1 Each party agrees to protect the Confidential Information of the other party and
not disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent.

4.2 Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period
of five (5) years.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 5 – Intellectual Property

5.1 All intellectual property developed by the Service Provider in connection with the
Services shall remain the exclusive property of the Service Provider unless otherwise
agreed.

5.2 The Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use deliverables
solely for internal business purposes.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 6 – Data Protection and Security

6.1 Each party shall comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

6.2 The Service Provider shall implement reasonable technical and organizational
measures to safeguard Client data against unauthorized access.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 7 – Representations and Warranties

7.1 Each party represents that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

7.2 The Service Provider warrants that the Services will be performed in a professional
and workmanlike manner.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 8 – Indemnification

8.1 The Service Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client against any claims
arising from breach of confidentiality or intellectual property infringement.

8.2 The Client shall indemnify the Service Provider against claims arising from misuse
of the Services.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 9 – Limitation of Liability

9.1 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

9.2 The total liability of either party shall not exceed the total fees paid under this
Agreement in the preceding twelve (12) months.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 10 – Term and Termination

10.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period
of three (3) years unless terminated earlier.

10.2 Termination for Convenience  
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by providing thirty (30)
days’ prior written notice to the other party.

10.3 Termination for Breach  
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately if the other party materially
breaches any provision and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of written
notice.

10.4 Effect of Termination  
Upon termination, all outstanding fees shall become immediately due and payable.
Confidentiality and intellectual property provisions shall survive termination.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 11 – Force Majeure

11.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable
control, including natural disasters, government actions, or labor disputes.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 12 – Governing Law and Jurisdiction

12.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
of India.

12.2 Courts located in Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction over disputes arising
under this Agreement.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 13 – Dispute Resolution

13.1 Any dispute shall first be resolved through good-faith negotiations.

13.2 If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration in accordance with the
Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 14 – Miscellaneous

14.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

14.2 Any amendments must be made in writing and signed by both parties.

14.3 Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver.

------------------------------------------------------------------

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first
written above.
